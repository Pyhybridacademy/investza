{{ PLATFORM_NAME }} is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), Act 4 of 2013 of South Africa. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Full name, date of birth, South African ID number or passport number
  • Email address and phone number
  • Residential address
  • Profile photograph (optional)

Financial Information

  • Bank account details (for deposits and withdrawals)
  • Cryptocurrency wallet addresses
  • Transaction history and investment activity
  • Proof of payment documents

Technical Information

  • IP address, browser type, and device information
  • Login timestamps and session activity
  • Platform usage and navigation data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Create and manage your investment account
  • Verify your identity (KYC/AML compliance)
  • Process deposits, investments, and withdrawals
  • Send transaction confirmations and account notifications
  • Comply with South African financial regulations
  • Detect and prevent fraud, money laundering, and suspicious activity
  • Improve our platform and user experience
  • Send marketing communications (only with your consent)

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Regulatory authorities: As required by FICA, SARS, and other SA regulatory bodies
  • Banking partners: To process transactions and verify bank accounts
  • Identity verification services: To complete KYC checks
  • Service providers: Email, hosting, and analytics providers bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Law enforcement: Where required by law or court order

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive financial data
  • Two-factor authentication options
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Strict access controls — staff access is role-based and logged

Despite our best efforts, no system is completely secure. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active and for a minimum of 5 years after account closure, as required by South African financial regulations (FICA). Transaction records are retained for a minimum of 7 years.

After the retention period, your data will be securely deleted or anonymised.

6. Your Rights Under POPIA

As a South African data subject, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your information for marketing purposes
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

To exercise your rights, contact our Information Officer at {{ SUPPORT_EMAIL }}.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and keep you logged in. We may also use analytics cookies to understand how you use our platform. You can disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect platform functionality.
